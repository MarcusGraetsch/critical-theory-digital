## Comparison: MySpace Collapse Lessons

### What Went Wrong (2008-2010)
1. **Product stagnation** — Failed to innovate
2. **User experience** — Cluttered, slow
3. **Facebook competition** — Cleaner alternative
4. **Parent company** (News Corp) — Misunderstood social

### Current Parallels?
- X: Product changes alienating users
- Meta: Heavy metaverse investment, core product aging
- TikTok: Regulatory pressure

### Key Difference
Modern platforms have **network effect moats** stronger than MySpace era:
- WhatsApp: Global standard for messaging
- Instagram: Creator economy lock-in
- LinkedIn: Professional network effects
